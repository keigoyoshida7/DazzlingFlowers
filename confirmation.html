Patronage
The spread of the coronavirus has caused suffering around the world, and festivals are often cancelled, making it impossible to realize the essential and cultural happiness of human beings.
In such a situation, we will rise up and once again promote festivals around the world to restore essential cultural happiness. To achieve this, our dream for this project is to start by organizing our own festivals, and to provide support to festival organizers who are currently in need all over the world. As a first step in realizing this dream, we will support festivals in Kyoto, Japan, which is in crisis due to the spread of the coronavirus, through the proceeds of our sales. We will also be sharing this dream on social networking sites and in our community to make this happen together with you. This is an activity for every person in the world to be a dazzling flower and celebrate under the dazzling flower.
