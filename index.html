CONCEPT

Dazzling Flowers produces content such as NFT and metaverse to create communities where people around the world can connect in peace. As our activities gain momentum, we will organize festivals online and offline in the hope of peace around the world!

BENEFIT

Dazzling flowers provide 7,000 + limited NFTs. Their buyers are free to add a 3-sentence theme to their work, which will be stored in the gallery and will be entitled to display on our official SNS or exhibit in the metaverse based on that 3-sentence theme through judging.

ERC721A

Dazzling Flowers utilizes the ERC721A. This allows us to offer low-cost, high-quality NFT by reducing the cost of minting and significantly reducing the cost of gas.

COMMUNITY

Dazzling Flowers will create a community where people from all over the world can gather together in peace using Discord, sandbox, twitter, minecraft, etc. Come and join us!

Metaverse

We will create a cityscape where the land within the sandbox will be festivals. We are planning to organize events, distribute NFT and offer special games to play to earn. We will also sell and give away skins that are in line with our NFT concept.

Minecraft

We will create a cityscape within Minecraft where festivals will take place and create a community where people from all over the world can feel connected. We are also planning to distribute free skins that fit the NFT concept. We are looking for engineers, creators, and artists who can grow the project together. Please contact us on our channel in the hope discord.

Patronage

The spread of the coronavirus has caused suffering around the world, and festivals are often cancelled, making it impossible to realize the essential and cultural happiness of human beings.
In such a situation, we will rise up and once again promote festivals around the world to restore essential cultural happiness. To achieve this, our dream for this project is to start by organizing our own festivals, and to provide support to festival organizers who are currently in need all over the world. As a first step in realizing this dream, we will support festivals in Kyoto, Japan, which is in crisis due to the spread of the coronavirus, through the proceeds of our sales. We will also be sharing this dream on social networking sites and in our community to make this happen together with you. This is an activity for every person in the world to be a dazzling flower and celebrate under the dazzling flower.
